STARDUST

This is a simple JS program to play around with. Just move the mouse around to repell stardust. You can change the RGB Colour, amount of stardust, and other parameters below. Have fun (•ᴗ•)


colourR(Original:64)
colourG(Original:255)
colourB(Original:255)
quantity(Max:10000)
velocityMultiply(default:0.5)
controlRange(default:0.1)
